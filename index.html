About

A pioneer at Etihad Airways since its inception in 2003, leading transformative growth and strategic initiatives that shaped the airline into a global aviation leader.

Niyas Ibrahim was a pioneer at Etihad Airways since its inception in 2003 and embarked on a remarkable journey of growth within the company, progressing into senior leadership roles, managing the airline's home base Hub at Abu Dhabi International Airport.

As he progressed within the organization, Niyas assumed senior leadership responsibilities and spearheaded strategic initiatives that propelled Etihad Airways as the world's leading airline. His visionary mindset and ability to navigate complex challenges in the hub allowed him to make significant contributions to the airline's sustainable growth.

As an integral member of the senior leadership team, he actively collaborated on the strategic acquisitions of partner airlines, AMADEUS, SABRE, ADAS ground handling company, Cargo & Catering company in Abu Dhabi that continued to shape the future of Etihad Airways.

As a seasoned aviation professional, Niyas Ibrahim embodies the values of innovation, integrity, and excellence. His experience and leadership make him a trusted and influential figure in the industry, and his passion for driving positive change sets him apart as an exceptional leader.
